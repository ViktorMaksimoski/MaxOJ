<SectionTitle>Solution</SectionTitle>

<SectionContent>
	Let $$dp[S][i]$$ be the number of routes that visit all cities in
	the subset $$S$$ and end at the city $$i$$. Then the transition will be:

	$$
	dp[S][i] = \sum_{x \in adj[i]} dp[S \setminus \{i\}][x] \quad \text{if } x \in S
	$$

	where $$S \setminus  {i}$$ is the subset $$S$$ without city $$i$$.
</SectionContent>
